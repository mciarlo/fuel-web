Turning a passion for health into an app for all ages & goals

Growing up I was always embarrassed by my weight and faced teasing from other kids. In high school I started to learn about nutrition and started using my Mom’s BowFlex.

Today I’ve completely changed me relationship to food and to my body. I made Fuel to do something no other app did for me: allow me to enjoy a slice of cake or some pizza and still challenge myself at the gym.

The carrot cake above? I enjoy a slice from my favorite bakery almost every week. I’ve learned how to balance my diet and still eat my favorite foods. Fuel helps me monitor my sugar, carbs, and calories so that when I want something sweet, I can do so guilt-free.

I finally found the perfect balance for my goals - mentally and physically. I know Fuel can do the same for you!
