Features

Fuel is everything you need to build the perfect health and nutrition companion. Stay on top of your goals with intelligent features that keep you mindful and on track.

  • A flexible calorie goal

    Track your calories and macronutrients with a minimum and maximum target instead of a fixed goal.

  • Radial visualization

    Visualize each day with the default bars or a stunning radial visualization.

  • 100% verified foods

    No junk data. New foods added weekly. All user submissions go through review before they’re added to search results.

  • Private by design

    No trackers, no ads, and no personal data collection. Ever. Your data stays on your device and within your private iCloud account. Search requests are completely anonymous. And subscription status checks use anonymous sessions.
